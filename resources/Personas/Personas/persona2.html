Detailed Description

Mihaiela Stanciu is a 31 years old woman who has always been career-oriented, managing to have her childhood dream job at a law firm working as a criminal defense attorney. Recently, she had a significant shift in her life, when she became a first-time mom. Due to her job, she is familiar with high-pressure moments but this is a completely new challenge for her. One of the most significant struggles Mihaiela deals with is the overwhelming sense of responsibility that comes with caring for a newborn, developing postpartum stress and anxiety because she feels like she isn’t doing a good job, always questioning her ability as a mother. Additionally, the lack of sleep and the isolation from her social life is leaving her exhausted and out of energy. She wants an app that could help her deal with all the stress she’s experiencing by trying new relaxing techniques, help her manage her time better and offer her a supportive community that could relate to her and advise her. Also, she would like to have an app where she could document and track this new experience for her while helping her get suggestions.

Scenarios

  • Morning(time management and tracking): Mihaiela uses the app in order to plan her whole day, making sure she doesn't forget anything.
  • Evening(sleep problems): Mihaiela uses the app in order to help her fall asleep quickly using specific exercises or sleep sounds if her baby had a long time falling asleep or even using those features in helping the baby go to sleep.
  • Free time: Mihaiela uses the app in order to relax or search new articles that could help her or answer her questions while also talking with communities about her struggles.
