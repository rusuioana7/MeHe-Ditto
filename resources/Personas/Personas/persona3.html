Detailed Description

Busuioc Maria, one of the leaders of a renowned law firm, embodies the epitome of dedication and expertise with her remarkable three-decade career. Despite the demands of her profession, Maria is deeply mindful of the welfare of her team members, recognizing the toll that the high-pressure environment can sometimes exact. Recently, she noticed subtle changes in two of her team members, Mihaiela and Mihai. Both had been struggling to manage the stress stemming from their workload, but Maria observed a positive shift in their demeanor and productivity. Mihaiela, once overwhelmed by the weight of her caseload, now navigates stressful situations with a newfound resilience and grace. Mihai, too, has exhibited remarkable growth, demonstrating increased focus and efficiency in managing his workload. Curious about how those changes happened, she had a discussion with both of them. This way she found out they both have been using MeHe, an Mental Health Companion App. The team members told her about how the breathing exercisies, stretching and meditation helped them with their burnout.
